Leadership scope today

Andrew's current role is best understood as the point where his impact shifted from individual and team-level delivery into broader software organizational leadership. He directs product and operations work across backend Python services, frontend services, QA automation, and cloud infrastructure, while advising adjacent software disciplines and helping keep multiple product lines aligned. His span of control includes the flagship customer portal, autoprovisioning services, contact center integrations, and the CI/CD and operational runbooks that keep carrier tenants online. He also sets technical direction, defines quarterly goals, and translates them into practical delivery plans that balance new feature work with ongoing stability.
